Kelly Ripa | Lifestyle And Vacations

Lifestyle

On the 2nd of October, 1970, Kelly Maria Ripa was born in Berlin, New Jersey, to parents Joseph and Esther Ripa.

Her father, Joseph Ripa, worked as a Democratic County clerk for Camden County, and her mother, Esther, was a homemaker.

Not many know about this, but Kelly is a professional ballet dancer. She also loves listening to music and played the piano growing up.

She did her early schooling at Eastern Regional High School in New Jersey and also starred in local theater productions.

Later, she attended Camden County College. However, she dropped her college to pursue acting and moved to New York City.

As of December 2023, Kelly Ripa’s net worth is an astounding $120 million, and she lives lavishly.

Moreover, Live with Kelly and Ryan’s co-host, Kelly Ripa, met her now-husband and then co-star, Mark Consuelos, on the set of All My Children back in 1995. The stunning pair took their vows on the 1st of May, 1996.

The two have three children together, namely Michael Joseph Consuelos (born 1997), Lola Grace Consuelos (born 2001), and Joaquin Antonio Consuelos (born 2003).

Recently on July 2021, she was seen enjoying a vacation on the East end of Long Island with her family, taking a break from her show, Live with Kelly and Ryan.

Similarly, Kelly, along with her husband Mark, often takes an escape from the city, spending time at their Hampton home.

Well, if you glance at Kelly’s social media handles, it seems that she loves to spend her spare time with family making good memories.

Kelly Ripa | Shows & Business

Shows

As a TV presenter, Kelly began to co-host the morning talk show, Live With Regis and Kelly, in 2001. The show soon attracted many new fans because of the excellent chemistry between Kelly and her co-star Regis Philbin.

With top-class ratings, the show’s viewership averaged around 6 million viewers per day.

In January 2011, Regis retired, and Kelly became the head of the show. The show’s name was renamed Live! with Kelly and had multiple strings of guest co-hosts.

In 2012 Michael Strahan joined the show. However, he left in 2016, and showrunners found Ryan Seacrest as the new co-host for the show in 2017.

Finally, the show’s title was renamed Live! With Kelly and Ryan.

Although many male co-hosts left the talk show eventually, Kelly seems determined to continue her work.

For her work on Live! with Kelly, she won a Daytime Emmy Award for “Outstanding Talk Show Host” in the years 2001, 2011, 2012, 2015, and 2016.

Business

Actress and TV host Kelly Ripa’s net worth is an astonishing $120 million as of December 2023.

Along with her husband, Mark, Kelly owns Milojo Productions, a production company based out of New York.

How the name, you ask? Very interestingly, the name clearly is taken from the first two letters of each of the pair’s three children Michael, Lola, and Joaquin.

Some of the popular projects of Milojo Productions include Dirty Soap on channel E!, Cheer! on channel CMT, Homemade Millionaire on channel TLC, and many more.

Recently, the production company worked on an unscripted true-crime series with Oxygen TV network.

Moreover, Kelly has endorsed several brands over the years, including Tide, 7 UP, and Pantene, among others.

Also, she worked as a voiceover artist for projects including Delgo, Kim Possible, and Fly Me to the Moon.

Multitalented Kelly also has a quite handsome number of film credits to her name. The popular one includes her role alongside Meryl Streep and Leonardo DiCaprio in 1996’s acclaimed Marvin’s Room.

Kelly Ripa | Career

Ripa started her early career when she began to get local TV gigs. Finally, in 1986, she got her first national TV exposure as a regular dancer.

Eventually, this led her to a job on Dance Party USA, quite a popular show.

As an actress, Kelly landed her first role on the ABC soap opera All My Children playing party girl Hayley Vaughn Santos. The soap opera ran from 1990 to 2002.

All My Children garnered five Soap Opera Digest Awards for Ripa. Additionally, she also earned three Daytime Emmy Award nominations.

Kelly was honored with a star in Hollywood Walk of Fame in 2015.
