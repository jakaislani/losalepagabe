Maude Apatow: Facts About Judd Apatows Celebrity Daughter

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, May 18, 2024

Maude Apatow, the daughter of filmmaker Judd Apatow and actress Leslie Mann, has achieved success in the entertainment industry.

She started her career with minor roles in her father’s films, benefiting from growing up in an entertainment-oriented household.

However, her breakthrough came in 2016 when she received recognition and critical acclaim for her role in the film “Other People.”

In 2017, Maude explored new creative avenues by acting in projects such as “The House of Tomorrow” and making her directorial debut with the short film “Don’t Mind Alice.”

These endeavors showcased her versatility and creativity, both in front of and behind the camera.

Notably, Maude garnered further acclaim for her portrayal of Lexi Howard in the HBO series “Euphoria.”

Her performance in the show received praise and solidified her reputation as a talented actress in the industry.

Overall, Maude Apatow has established herself as a noteworthy figure in acting and directing.

With her early exposure to the entertainment world and remarkable performances, she continues to pursue a promising and successful career.

Maude Apatow’s Net Worth

In 2023, Maude Apatow’s net worth is approximately $25 million, mainly earned through her acting career.

She has starred in various movies and TV series, such as “Euphoria,” “The Bubble,” and “The House of Tomorrow.”

Additionally, she has ventured into directing and producing several short films.

Maude Apatow’s Family

Maude comes from a talented family in the entertainment industry. Her parents are filmmaker Judd Apatow and actress Leslie Mann.

She has a younger sister named Iris Apatow, who is also part of their creative family.

Judd Apatow

Judd Apatow, an American filmmaker, comedian, and actor, is renowned for his work in comedy and drama films.

He founded Apatow Productions and has produced and directed notable films such as;

  • The 40-Year-Old Virgin” (2005)
  • Knocked Up” (2007)
  • Funny People” (2009)
  • This Is 40″ (2012)
  • Trainwreck” (2015)
  • The King of Staten Island” (2020)
  • The Bubble” (2022) and more…

Apatow’s films have achieved a global box office gross of over $2 billion.

He has received numerous awards for his work, including two Primetime Emmy Awards for Outstanding Comedy Series for “Freaks and Geeks” and “Girls.”

Furthermore, he received an Academy Award nomination for Best Original Screenplay for “Knocked Up.”

Leslie Mann

Leslie Mann, an American actress, gained recognition for her performances in various films. Some notable ones include The Cable Guy, Knocked Up, This Is 40, and The Change-Up.

Additionally, she showcased her talent in television series like Freaks and Geeks and The Kominsky Method.

Iris Apatow

Iris Apatow, an American actress, is the daughter of filmmaker Judd Apatow and actress Leslie Mann.

She has showcased her talent in movies like Assassination Nation, The King of Staten Island, and The Bubble, as well as the television series Love.

Renowned for her dry wit and adeptness at portraying multifaceted and relatable characters, Iris Apatow is a rising star in Hollywood.

Her career is destined to thrive in the future.
