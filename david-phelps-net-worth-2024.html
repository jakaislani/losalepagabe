David Phelps’ Early Life and Career

David Phelps was born in Texas, United States, and showed an interest in music from a young age. He attended Baylor University, where he studied music and voice. After graduating, he joined the Gaither Vocal Band, where he gained recognition for his impressive vocal range and ability to hold long notes.

Phelps’ tenure with the Gaither Vocal Band significantly contributed to his net worth. The band has released numerous albums, many of which have achieved gold and platinum status. Their concerts and tours have also been highly successful, further boosting Phelps’ earnings.

David Phelps’ Solo Career

Phelps left the Gaither Vocal Band in 2005 to focus on his solo career. His solo albums have been well-received, with several reaching the top of the Billboard Christian Albums chart. His concerts and tours as a solo artist have also been successful, contributing significantly to his net worth.

Income from Songwriting

Real Estate Investments
