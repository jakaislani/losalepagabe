Nick Kyrgios embodies both an amusing and an angry young sportsperson.

Nick Kyrgios is the winner of six ATP championships, is one of the most engaging sports figures, and his matches are always exciting to watch. The man with the incredibly unique and interesting hitting style is also admired for his honesty, as he always speaks his heart. His fantastic replies and punchline had the crowd in stitches. He has a furious side in addition to his hilarious side. Nick headlines for breaching court behavior, for which he has been fined and suspended by ATP. He may be seen breaking rockets and chairs, slamming bottles, yelling, and spitting. During Wimbledon, he confessed to hitting 19-time Grand Slam champion Rafael Nadal with a ball in his chest.

Marriage between cultures

Giorgos’s marriage is a cross-cultural union. He married Norlaila, a Gombak, Selangor native, as a Greek-born man. Norlaila left Malaysia in her twenties to work as a computer engineer in Australia. They are a happily married pair despite their cultural differences.

His wife is from a Royal family.

The Sultan of Pahang, Norlaila’s grandfather’s cousin, was the title used by the rulers of the former Pahang Sultanate. As a result of the relationship, she is elevated to the position of Tengku of Pahang. They have a special relationship despite coming from two distinct backgrounds and can be seen together practically everywhere.

The couple has three kids.

Giorgos and Norlaila are parents of three children. Nick is the youngest of their three children. Nick was born on April 27, 1995, making him 25 years old. Their children have taken a different professional path and succeeded in their fields. Christos, the other son, is a lawyer, while Halimah, the daughter, is an actress, model, and dancer. The three siblings have an incredible relationship. They may be seen cheering for their brother Nick in practically every game. Dustin Brown, a Jamaican-German professional tennis player, is Halimah’s boyfriend.

Net worth

Giorgos Kyrgios’s net worth is about $2 million as of December 2023. On the other hand, his kid has made a good living from his profession. Despite being at the center of controversy in the tennis world and having to pay large fines for his misbehavior, no one can dispute that he is a clever player with the capacity to win Grand Slams. According to the ATP, he is rated 42nd in the world in men’s singles and the second-highest ranked Australian as of September 2020. Giorgos feels that his kid has a bright future and that he has a strong support system in place to help him succeed.
