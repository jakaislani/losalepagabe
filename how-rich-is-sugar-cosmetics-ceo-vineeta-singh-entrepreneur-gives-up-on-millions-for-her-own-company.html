How Rich Is Sugar Cosmetics CEO Vineeta Singh? Entrepreneur Gives Up On Millions For Her Own Company

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, May 18, 2024

How rich is Sugar Cosmetics CEO Vineeta Singh? She once turned down a 10 million Rupees or a $133,000 job offer to establish her cosmetic brand.

Vineeta Singh is an entrepreneur and a businesswoman from India.

Vineeta is the CEO and co-founder of SUGAR Cosmetics, an Indian cosmetic brand.

The brand is now emerging as one of the leading cosmetic brands competing with other major cosmetic brands.

Besides India, SUGAR Cosmetics now is manufactured in state-of-the-art manufacturing plants in Germany, Italy, America, and South Korea.

With that, the Indian cosmetic market has emerged all over the world.

Following the success of her brand, she has now become a popular figure in India, and now she is one of the judges in India’s version of Shark Tank.

How Rich Is Sugar Cosmetics CEO Vineeta Singh? Net Worth Explored

Sugar Cosmetic CEO Vineeta Singh estimated net worth is $8 million, as per dailynewscatcher.

However, this is figure might be conflictual as Vineeta has not officially disclosed her properties and assets details to the public.

The 30-year-old is the CEO and co-founder of Sugar Cosmetic, the cosmetic company she founded in 2015, alongside her husband and business partner, Kaushik Mukherjee.

However, Sugar is not her first attempt to establish a business.

Singh first tried her hand on two other e-commerce businesses, which turn out o be a failure for which she turned down a ten million rupees job offer.

Now, her cosmetic brand’s sales are over $265,000 every day, according to Forbes India.

Currently, Singh’s brand is sold over 2,500 plus outlets in over 130 cities across India.

Following its massive success, the company set a revenue of nearly $14 million in the fiscal year of 2020. As of now, the company is valued at nearly $100 million.

Sugar Cosmetics CEO Vineeta Singh Wikipedia Explored

Sugar Cosmetics CEO Vineeta Singh is not available on Wikipedia.

Despite her success as a woman entrepreneur, Vineeta does not have an official Wiki, which is unfortunate.

However, her personal information is available on various websites all over the internet.

So, let’s dig into her biography.

Singh is an Indian national, born and raised in Delhi, where she did her schooling at Delhi Public School and R.K Puram.

As per her higher education, she graduated from India’s most prestigious education institute IIT Madras in electrical engineering.

Later on, she got her MBA from IIM in Ahemdabad.

Currently, the 30-year-old serves as the CEO of SUGAR Cosmetic, the company she founded with her husband, Kaushik Mukherjee.

Vineeta and Kaushik are the parents of two sons.
