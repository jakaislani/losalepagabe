Wiki/Biography

Sharron Townsend was born on Wednesday, 17 August 1994 (age 26 years; as in 2020) in Camden, New Jersey.

Sharron Townsend As a Child

He moved to Hershey, Pennsylvania, and studied at Milton Hersey School, Hershey, Pennsylvania. He was the president of his school’s National Honor Society. He did his Bachelor’s in Economy from Franklin & Marshall College, Lancaster, Pennsylvania.

Relationships

He came in a relationship with his co-contestant in the show “Too Hot To Handle,” Rhonda Paul.

Rhonda Paul and Sharron Townsend

They separated after the show because of being in a long-distance relationship. Talking about his breakup with Rhonda, he said,

I was having family issues. My mom was sick. I ended up closing off again. Not intentionally, but I found myself distancing from her.”

Career

Sharron Townsend is a professional trainer and kids wrestling coach. He did modelling for Aja Martin Photography for more than 5 years. He also modelled for the fashion brands, Plugged N Co and Dirty Politics.

Sharron Townsend in an Advertisement for Dirty Politics

In 2017, he appeared in an episode of the show “Undressed,” which was aired on MTV. In the same year, he also tried his luck in American Ninja Warrior, a sports entertainment competition, but wasn’t selected in it. He made an appearance in the music video of “Why” by Angelica Villa ft Jacquees.

Later, he appeared in the music video, ‘Shea Butter Baby’ by Ari Lennox and J. Cole and ‘Fresh N Clean’ by Fetty Wap.
