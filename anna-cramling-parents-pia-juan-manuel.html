Anna Cramling is a Spanish-Swedish chess player and YouTuber. She holds the FIDE title of Woman FIDE Master (WFM). Likewise, Anna has represented Sweden in many championships.

She played for her nation in Chess Olympiad (2016, 2022) and two European Team Chess Championships. In addition to that, Cramling is also a Twitch streamer who began streaming in early 2020.

Initially, Cramling focused on chess content. Also, her Father and mother have appeared in her live stream. As of now, her YouTube channel has also collected more than 433k subscribers.

Anna Cramling Parents: Pia Cramling and Juan Manuel Bellón López

Pia Cramling (Mother) and Juan Manuel Bellón López (Father) are the proud parents of Anna Cramling. They gave birth to their daughter Anna on April 30, 2002, in Málaga, Spain.

It can be said that Anna got inspired by her parents to get involved in this field, as both of his parents are renowned chess players. Her mother, Pia, became the fifth woman to earn the FIDE title of Grandmaster (GM) in 1992.

Since the early 1980s, Pia has been one of the strongest female players in the world and the highest-rated female in the FIDE World Rankings on three events. 

On the other hand, her Father, Juan, holds the FIDE title of Grandmaster (1978). He has been active in this sport for a long time and has taken home multiple titles.

Also, he was a five-time Spanish Chess Champion, Chess Olympiad individual silver medal winner, and European Team Chess Championship individual bronze medal winner in 1989.
