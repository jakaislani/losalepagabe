Are Billie Eilish And Jesse Rutherford Dating?

Even though Jesse and Billie have only been seen together a few times, it is not clear if they are dating or not. Also, neither singer has confirmed rumors that they are dating. But the pictures of them kissing in L.A. are proof in and of themselves!

Who Is Jesse Rutherford?

Jesse is a singer-songwriter who is best known for being the lead singer of the alternative rock band The Neighbourhood. Together with the rest of his band, Jesse wrote “Sweater Weather,” which topped the Billboard Alternative Songs chart and went 5 Platinum in the USA in 2020.

Jesse has been in the entertainment business since he was a child. He has been in commercials and a few movies, like Bundy in 2002 and Life or Something Like It. He also had a small part in the episode “Marauders” of Star Trek: Enterprise.

As a teenager, he was the lead singer of several bands. In 2011, he put out a mixtape called “Truth Hurts, Truth Heals,” which inspired him and other band members to start The Neighborhood. The band’s first song, “Female Robbery,” came out in 2012. It was followed by “Sweater Weather,” both of which were on their first studio album, “I Love You.”
