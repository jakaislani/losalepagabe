Kirill Kaprizov is a Russian professional ice hockey forward for the Minnesota Wild of the National Hockey League.

Kirill Olegovich Kaprizov was born on April 26, 1997, in Novokuznetsk, Russia to parents, Natalya Kaprizova and Oleg Kaprizov.

Kirill Kaprizov made his Kontinental Hockey League debut playing with Metallurg Novokuznetsk during the 2014–15 KHL season, after being drafted first overall in the 2014 KHL Junior Draft.

Kirill Kaprizov was drafted by the Minnesota Wild of the National Hockey League in the fifth round of the 2015 NHL Entry Draft, 135th overall. He won the Calder Memorial Trophy as NHL rookie of the year in 2021, becoming the first Wild player to win the award.

Kirill Kaprizov was the youngest player in the Russian team at the 2018 Winter Olympics in Pyeongchang, South Korea.

Is Kirill Kaprizov Married?

No, Kirill Kaprizov has never been married. There is no readily available information about his past relationship and anyone to whom he was previously engaged.
