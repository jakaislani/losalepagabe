Lisa Kudrow

Lisa Kudrow is an American singer, actress, comedian, writer, and producer. She is best known for her role as Phoebe Buffay in the hit TV show Friends.

Lisa Kudrow’s net worth is estimated to be $90 million in 2023. Her primary source of income comes from film, television, and commercial projects. Lisa has not only made money, but she has also gained global recognition, love, and appreciation from viewers and critics.

Where was Lisa Kudrow born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Lisa Kudrow was born on July 30, 1963, and will be 58 in 2023. She was born and raised in Los Angeles, California, USA, in an upper-middle-class family. She is of American origin and follows the Jewish faith. Lisa completed her elementary education at Tarzana’s Portola Middle School.

She then went to Taft High School in Woodland Hills, Los Angeles, where she graduated. She also attended Vassar College and graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Biology. Lisa aspired to be a headache specialist like her father, a doctor. She spent several years working for her father before deciding to pursue her passions of acting and singing.
